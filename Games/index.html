A Selection Of In-Browser Games
2048

It's a type of “sliding block puzzle” — think Threes!, on which 2048 is based, or the old-timey game klotski — that's played on an almost Sudoku-like grid. Like Sudoku, it also involves some math. The object of the game is to combine the numbers displayed on the tiles until you reach 2048.

Cookie Clicker

Cookie Clicker is an incremental web-browser game developed by Orteil. The goal of the game is to produce as many cookies (by clicking on the Big Cookie and buying buildings to produce them) as you can.

Doge 2048

A Classic Game Of 2048, but with a doge style twist

Doge Miner

DogeMiner: Mine Dogecoins and buy doge companions to help you in your quest to the moon!

Duck Life 3

Duck Life 3: Own your very own genetically modified duck, train, race and dominate the competition

Duck Life 4

Duck Life 4 is a duck racing game set after the ban on genetically modified ducks. A year has passed since the ban on genetically modified ducks and now it's up to you to defeat the world champion. Train your duck team to compete in six new locations around the world.

Chrome Dinosaur Game

No internet? No problem! Play this little dinosaur game even if it is blocked for you! What will be your highscore? Now with PWA support!

Flappy Bird

Click on the screen, or use your spacebar to get started. Fly the bird as far as you can without hitting a pipe. If you do you lose, how far can you go?

Flash Tetris

Play the tetris port made by Paul Neave! (Flash Tetris is an early version of Tetris N-Blox!)

Meme 2048

A classic game of 2048, but in a funny style

MotoX3M

Hop on your dirt bike and ride over jumps, do tricks, and try not to fly off! Memorize the course and execute your stunts perfectly.

MotoX3M Pool Party

Moto X3M Pool Party is a motorbike racing game with "summer" as main theme. This new game in the series has 22 new challenging levels.

MotoX3M Spooky Land

Moto X3M Spooky Land is a game with a Halloween theme. Do front and backflips, try to the finish line as fast as you can.

MotoX3M Winter

Race your motorcycle across ice and snow to reach the finish line in this game. Get some big air and pull off sweet flips and tricks to save time.

Subway Surfers

Subway Surfers is a classic endless runner game. You play as Jake, who surfs the subways and tries to escape from the grumpy Inspector and his dog.

There Is No Game

What ever you do, do not click on the title. There is nothing at all. Please play the game below or above me. It is way better.

Temple Run 2

Play one of the top mobile games made by Imangi Studios right in your browser! Run as you are being chased, how far will you go?
