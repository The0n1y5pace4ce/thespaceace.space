A Selection Of In-Browser Games
2048

It's a type of “sliding block puzzle” — think Threes!, on which 2048 is based, or the old-timey game klotski — that's played on an almost Sudoku-like grid. Like Sudoku, it also involves some math. The object of the game is to combine the numbers displayed on the tiles until you reach 2048.

Cookie Clicker

Cookie Clicker is an incremental web-browser game developed by Orteil. The goal of the game is to produce as many cookies (by clicking on the Big Cookie and buying buildings to produce them) as you can.

Doge 2048

A Classic Game Of 2048, but with a doge style twist

Doge Miner

DogeMiner: Mine Dogecoins and buy doge companions to help you in your quest to the moon!

Duck Life 3

Duck Life 3: Own your very own genetically modified duck, train, race and dominate the competition

Duck Life 4

Duck Life 4 is a duck racing game set after the ban on genetically modified ducks. A year has passed since the ban on genetically modified ducks and now it's up to you to defeat the world champion. Train your duck team to compete in six new locations around the world.
